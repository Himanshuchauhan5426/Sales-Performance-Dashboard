# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This project is a Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard designed to provide business insights into sales, profit, orders, market performance, and product category trends.

The dashboard enables users to analyze sales data across different markets, categories, sub-categories, and years using interactive visualizations and filters.

---

## Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Profit: 1.47M
- Total Sales: 12.64M
- Total Orders: 26K
- Profit Margin: 0.12

### Interactive Filters
- Sub-Category Filter
- Order Date Filter
- Category Filter

### Visualizations

#### 1. Sales by Category and Market
- Stacked Column Chart
- Compare sales across:
  - Technology
  - Furniture
  - Office Supplies
- Market-wise breakdown:
  - Africa
  - Asia Pacific
  - Europe
  - LATAM
  - USCA

#### 2. Profit by Sub-Category
- Pie Chart
- Analyze profit contribution from different product sub-categories.

#### 3. Sales by Year and Category
- Line Chart
- Trend analysis from 2012–2015.
- Compare category growth over time.

#### 4. Country Count by Market
- Donut Chart
- Market distribution analysis.

#### 5. Detailed Sales Table
- Quantity Sold
- Total Sales
- Sub-category Performance
